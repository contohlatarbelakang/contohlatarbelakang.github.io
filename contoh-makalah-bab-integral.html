20. bab integral tak tentu , tolong bantuanya


Gunakan metode substitusi,
[tex]u = \ln (x + 1) \\ \frac{du}{dx} = \frac{1}{x + 1} \\ (x + 1)du = dx[/tex]
Substitusikan ke integral awal,
[tex]\int \frac{\ln(x + 1)}{x + 1} dx = \int \frac{u}{x + 1} (x + 1)du \\ = \int{u}du \\ = \frac{1}{2} {u}^{2} + c \\ = \frac{1}{2} (\ln(x + 1) ) ^{2} + c[/tex]
