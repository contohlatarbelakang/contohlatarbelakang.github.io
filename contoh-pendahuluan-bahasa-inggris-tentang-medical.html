13. Definisi dari (bahasa inggris) - Purchase form - Order form - Withdrawal slip - Deposit slip - Reservation form - Medical form


Answer:

1. Purchase form

Purchase form is also known as purchase order (PO) form. Purchase form is a commercial document which is made by a buyer to a seller and used as a request to purchase products or services. This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products or service purchased.

2. Order form

Order form is a commercial document which is filled in by customers and used to order goods from a company. This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products ordered.

3. Withdrawal slip

Withdrawal slip is a piece of paper which is filled in by a client before withdrawing cash from an account. This slip is generally used in a bank. This slip contains the date of withdrawal, the client's name, the client's account number, the client's signature, and the amount of withdrawal.

4. Deposit slip

Deposit slip is a piece of paper which is filled in by a client before depositing cash into an account. This slip is generally used in a bank, too. This slip contains the date of depositing, the client's name, the client's account number, the amout of money deposited, and the client's signature.

5. Reservation form

Reservation form is a form which is filled in when booking or reserving something. it can be a hotel room, entertainment tickets, transportation, etc. This form contains the client's information, a number of things or places booked, and the date and time of when it's used. Some reservation forms requires down payment as a reservation deal.

6. Medical form

Medical form is a form which is filled in by a patient to a doctor. This form contains the patient's personal health information, such as his/her illness, allergies, currently taken medicines, family health history, etc.

I hope the answer is clear enough and meets your expectation.

"Pada suatu pagi cerah di kota kecil, Sarah terbangun dengan rasa tidak enak di tubuhnya. Dia merasakan demam tinggi, sakit kepala yang menyengat, dan tubuhnya terasa lemas. Khawatir dengan kondisinya, Sarah memutuskan untuk pergi ke pusat kesehatan setempat.

Setibanya di pusat kesehatan, Sarah disambut oleh seorang perawat yang ramah. Perawat tersebut meminta Sarah untuk menjalani pemeriksaan awal, termasuk pengukuran suhu tubuh dan tekanan darah. Setelah itu, Sarah diminta untuk menunggu di ruang tunggu sambil menunggu giliran bertemu dengan dokter.

Saat gilirannya tiba, Sarah masuk ke ruang dokter dan menjelaskan semua gejala yang dirasakannya. Dokter dengan cermat mendengarkan keluhan Sarah dan melakukan pemeriksaan fisik lebih lanjut. Setelah melalui serangkaian tes dan analisis, dokter akhirnya mendiagnosis Sarah menderita infeksi saluran pernapasan atas.

Dokter memberikan penjelasan detail tentang kondisi Sarah dan memberikan rekomendasi perawatan yang tepat. Sarah diberikan obat untuk meredakan gejala dan instruksi tentang istirahat yang cukup, menjaga asupan cairan, dan menghindari aktivitas yang berat. Dokter juga memberikan informasi tambahan tentang cara menjaga kebersihan diri dan mencegah penyebaran infeksi kepada orang lain.

Pengalaman ini juga mengingatkannya akan pentingnya menjaga kesehatan dan mengambil langkah-langkah pencegahan untuk mencegah penyakit dan infeksi."

25. apa bahasa inggrisnya UKS selain School Medical Room sama Nursing?


In English, the term 'UKS (Unit Kesehatan Sekolah) is best translated as 'School's Health Clinic', beside School Medical Room and Nursing because such term is suitable for the meaning of it, not simply translated based on the words in the term of the original language itself.

Penjelasan:

In translation, the main things to consider are the context and proper vocabulary. Those two are essential so that the translated words in the target language will not confuse the native speaker of it. The literal and word-by-word is not highly recommended as it tends to make inaccurate meaning.

In its context, 'Unit Kesehatan Sekolah' is not a unit of organisation as it's more like the health section or facility run by the school for the students. It's mostly like a clinic inside the school. So, it is best to translated as 'School's health clinic'.In terms of vocabulary, the word 'clinic' is the most appropriate. The option in the task 'school medical room' has fairly closer meaning to it than 'nursing room' as the latter can have a different meaning, for mothers taking care of the their babies like breastfeeding or changing nappies.
