38. tolong bantu ya kakak" pidato yang tentang prakerin tapi yang dalam bahasa inggris


PRAKERIN (Industrial Work Practice) is an education, training and learning activities conducted in the business world or industry that are relevant to the competence (ability) of students according to their fields. In the implementation carried out by certain procedures, for students who aim to internship in a workplace, both the business world and the industrial world at least already have the basic skills in accordance with their field or have obtained the provision of school counselors to have the basic sciences to be applied in the world business or industrial world. The main reason why the students of cork have the basic knowledge base in the field so that in the implementation of the Industrial Work Practice does not experience significant obstacles in the application of basic science that is likely in the process of industrial work practices to get new sciences that are not taught in Vocational Institutions related.

The purpose of PRAKERIN is:    
1) Implementing materials that have been obtained in schools;    
2) Shaping a constructive mindset for students of PRAKERIN;    
3) Train students to communicate / interact professionally in the real world of work;    
4) Establish a good work ethic for PRAKERIN students;
5) Increase and develop basic knowledge owned by PRAKERIN students according to their respective fields;   
6) Increase the type of skills possessed by students to be developed and implemented in everyday life; and
7) Establish good cooperation between school and industry world.

The Obligations of Students in Prisoners' Places are:    
1) Carry out basic tasks and functions that have been agreed upon;    
2) Comply with every Instruction at work;    
3) Carrying out K3 (Health and Safety);    
4) To maintain the good reputation of the Education Institution (Almamater), the Business World and the Industrial World;    
5) Conducting Observation and Research with Positive Objectives; and
6) Ask the competent party if they are not understood.

In the process of filing PRAKERIN students are required to follow the procedures established by the institutions, the Industrial World and the Business World. Each institute, the Industrial World and the Business World of course have different procedures depending on the condition of each Industrial World and Business World related.

40. Cara membuat laporan prakerin???


1. Di bagian atas adalah judul dari laporan, dituliskan “Laporan Praktek Kerja    Industri”.

2. Setelah judul laporan, di bawahnya dituliskan nama tempat di mana kamu    melaksanakan prakerin, dituliskan pada contoh “Dinas Pendidikan             Kabupaten Cirebon”. Jangan lupa sisipkan kata penghubung Di- atau Pada.

3. Bagian ketiga, judul pembahasan laporan tersebut, mengenai apakah materi pembahasan yang kamu akan buat. Dalam contoh dituliskan “Pengelolaan Aset Pada Dinas Pendidikan Kabupaten Cirebon”.

4. Bagian keempat ada kalimat tambahan, sifatnya opsional. Namun, agar lebih efektif sebaiknya dituliskan saja, kalimat itu adalah teks pengajuan laporan. Contoh “Diajukan sebagai syarat untuk mengikuti Ujian Nasional dan Uji Kompetensi.”

5. Lalu dibagian kelima adalah logo lembaga pendidikan atau sekolah yang kamu tempati. Letak logo tepat di bagian tengah sampul.

6. Bagian keenam, tuliskan data diri kamu guna menandakan bahwa laporan tersebut hasil susunan dan milik kamu. Cukup tuliskan nama, nomor induk siswa, dan kelas.

7. Lalu yang terakhir pada bagian ketujuh adalah identitas pendek sekolah kamu. Cukup tuliskan jurusan di sekolah yang kamu tempuh, nama lembaga pendidikan atau sekolah, akreditasi dari sekolah tersebut, dan alamatnya.
