In English, the word "animation" literally means the act of giving life or motion to something, such as a drawing, model, or computer graphic. It is often used to refer to the process of creating a series of still images or frames that, when played back in rapid succession, create the illusion of movement and bring characters or objects to life. Animations can be created using a variety of techniques, including hand-drawn, stop-motion, and computer-generated methods.
